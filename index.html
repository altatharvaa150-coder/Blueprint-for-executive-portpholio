Academic Expertise

Research & Specialisation

Harjeet Jaggi's doctoral research at IIT Delhi placed him at the intersection of advanced physics and engineering materials — areas that demand both deep theoretical understanding and precise experimental methodology. His areas of specialisation include:

🔬

Materials Science

Advanced study of material structure, properties, and behaviour under physical conditions — with particular focus on characterisation techniques developed during doctoral research at IIT Delhi.

🧪

Polymer Engineering

In-depth research into the molecular architecture and mechanical behaviour of polymers, including synthesis, modification, and performance analysis of polymer systems relevant to industrial applications.

⚙️

Composite Materials

Study of multi-phase composite systems — examining how the combination of constituent materials produces properties superior to those of individual components, with applications in structural and advanced engineering domains.

⚛️

Applied Physics

Bridging fundamental physics theory with practical engineering application — including mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and optics — with a researcher's perspective grounded in IIT-level scientific rigour.

Credentials

Educational Background

Harjeet Jaggi holds a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) and a Master of Technology (M.Tech.) from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi — one of India's foremost centres of scientific and engineering research. His doctoral work focused on the advanced study of materials science, encompassing polymer engineering and composite material behaviour.

His undergraduate foundation was laid at the Cochin University of Science & Technology (CUSAT), from which he graduated with a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in 2009 — beginning a trajectory that would carry him to the pinnacle of India's academic establishment.

Career

The Journey

IIT Delhi

Doctoral Researcher — IIT Delhi

Admitted to IIT Delhi following his undergraduate studies, Harjeet Jaggi pursued and completed both M.Tech. and Ph.D. in materials science. This period of intensive research — characterised by rigorous experimental work, peer collaboration, and scientific writing — formed the intellectual core that defines his entire professional identity.

Faculty · Pune

Faculty — Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune

His transition from research to academic leadership began at MIT Pune, where he served as a faculty member. Teaching engineering undergraduates demanded the translation of doctoral-level knowledge into structured, accessible pedagogy — a skill that would define his approach to education for the next decade.

5 Years · Delhi

Senior Faculty — NEET Coaching Centre, Delhi

Five years in Delhi's most competitive educational environment — preparing students for NEET — sharpened his ability to rapidly identify individual learning gaps and respond with precision. This phase established him as an authority in high-stakes examination preparation at a national level.

Present · Pune

Founder — Teaching Institute, Kondhwa, Pune

Drawing on two decades of research and academic experience, Harjeet Jaggi founded his own teaching institute in Pune — an entrepreneurial undertaking driven by a conviction that research-grade intellectual rigour must be made accessible to every motivated student. The institute has since produced multiple IIT and AIIMS entrants, and continues to operate both in-person and online.

The Institute

In addition to his research and consulting work, Harjeet Jaggi founded and leads a specialised teaching institute in Kondhwa, Pune — focused on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics for Class 11–12 students and competitive examination candidates (JEE & NEET).
