Academic Expertise

Research & Specialisation

Harjeet Jaggi's doctoral research at IIT Delhi placed him at the intersection of advanced physics and engineering materials — areas demanding both deep theoretical understanding and precise experimental methodology.

Materials Science

Advanced study of material structure, properties, and behaviour under physical conditions — with particular focus on characterisation techniques developed during doctoral research at IIT Delhi.

Polymer Engineering

In-depth research into the molecular architecture and mechanical behaviour of polymers, including synthesis, modification, and performance analysis for industrial applications.

Composite Materials

Study of multi-phase composite systems — examining how the combination of constituent materials produces properties superior to individual components, with applications in structural engineering.

Applied Physics

Bridging fundamental physics theory with practical engineering application — including mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and optics — with a researcher's perspective grounded in IIT-level rigour.

Credentials

Educational Background

Harjeet Jaggi holds a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) and a Master of Technology (M.Tech.) from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi — one of India's foremost centres of scientific and engineering research. His doctoral work focused on the advanced study of materials science, encompassing polymer engineering and composite material behaviour.

His undergraduate foundation was laid at the Cochin University of Science & Technology (CUSAT), from which he graduated with a B.Tech. in 2009.

Career

The Journey

IIT Delhi

Doctoral Researcher — IIT Delhi

Admitted to IIT Delhi following undergraduate studies, Harjeet Jaggi pursued and completed both M.Tech. and Ph.D. in materials science — intensive research characterised by rigorous experimental work, peer collaboration, and scientific writing.

Faculty · Pune

Faculty — Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune

His first major academic leadership role — translating doctoral-level research into structured, accessible pedagogy for engineering undergraduates.

5 Years · Delhi

Senior Faculty — NEET Coaching Centre, Delhi

Five years in Delhi's most competitive examination coaching environment, establishing him as a national authority in high-stakes Physics preparation.

Present · Pune

Founder — Teaching Institute, Kondhwa, Pune

Drawing on two decades of research and academic experience, Harjeet Jaggi founded his own teaching institute in Pune — producing multiple IIT and AIIMS entrants, and operating both in-person and online.
