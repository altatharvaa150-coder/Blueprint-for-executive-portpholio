Educational Background

Harjeet Jaggi holds a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) and a Master of Technology (M.Tech.) from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi — one of India's foremost centres of scientific and engineering research. His doctoral work focused on the advanced study of materials science, encompassing polymer engineering and composite material behaviour.

His undergraduate foundation was laid at the Cochin University of Science & Technology (CUSAT), from which he graduated with a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in 2009 — beginning a trajectory that would carry him to the pinnacle of India's academic establishment.

Career

The Journey

2009 — 2011

Doctoral Research — IIT Delhi

Following the completion of his undergraduate studies, Harjeet Jaggi was admitted to IIT Delhi, where he pursued and completed both his M.Tech. and Ph.D. in materials science. This period of intensive research formed the intellectual foundation upon which his entire teaching practice is built — an understanding of science not merely as a subject to be taught, but as a discipline to be practised.

Faculty — Maharashtra

Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune

His first major teaching appointment was at the Maharashtra Institute of Technology (MIT), Pune, where he spent two years as faculty. Immersed in academia, he taught and mentored students of engineering and applied sciences — developing his pedagogical approach and learning to translate complex doctoral-level knowledge into clear, teachable concepts.

5 Years — Delhi

NEET Coaching Centre, Delhi

Seeking broader impact, he relocated to Delhi and joined a prominent NEET coaching centre, where he taught for five years. In this high-pressure, results-oriented environment, he refined his teaching methodologies — leveraging his understanding of Physics and Chemistry to help hundreds of aspiring medical students prepare for one of India's most competitive examinations. This experience sharpened his ability to identify each student's individual learning needs and address them with precision.

Present — Pune

Founder — Teaching Institute, Kondhwa, Pune

Driven by a conviction that transformative education must be both rigorous and personal, Harjeet Jaggi founded his own teaching institute in Kondhwa, Pune. The institute has since produced students who have gained entry to esteemed medical and engineering colleges across the country. Both online and in-person modes of learning are offered, ensuring accessibility for students from diverse backgrounds. Every session — whether virtual or in-person — is characterised by interactivity and conceptual depth, supported by whiteboards and PDF-based teaching tools.
