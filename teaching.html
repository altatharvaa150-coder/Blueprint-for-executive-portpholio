The Founding

From Researcher to Founder

After completing a Ph.D. at IIT Delhi, serving as faculty at MIT Pune, and spending five years as a senior physics educator at a national NEET coaching centre in Delhi, Harjeet Jaggi founded his own teaching institute in Kondhwa, Pune.

The institute is the practical expression of his academic philosophy: that every student — regardless of background — deserves access to the same intellectual standards upheld in the best research institutions in the country. This is not a coaching centre. It is a place where science is taught the way a researcher thinks about it.

Academic Scope

Subjects & Examinations

The institute focuses on three core subjects across senior secondary and competitive examination levels — taught with the rigour of a research laboratory.

⚛️

Physics

Mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, optics, and modern physics — for Class 11–12, IIT JEE (Mains & Advanced), and NEET-UG. Taught from first principles, not formulae.

🧪

Chemistry & Mathematics

Supporting subjects for Class 11–12 and JEE/NEET preparation — taught with the same conceptual depth as Physics. CBSE · ISC · IB · AS/A Levels.

💻

Online & In-Person

Live online sessions via Zoom — interactive, real-time, and available nationally. In-person sessions at the Kondhwa, Pune centre by appointment.
